## Product Summary

49 professional Lightroom presets covering technical workflow optimization and 2025-2026 creative trends. Organized into 4 tiered packages for maximum market reach.

## Target Markets

1. **Wedding & Portrait Photographers** — Creamy Moody, Light & Airy, Soft Pastel
2. **Instagram Influencers & Content Creators** — Matte Vintage/Modern, Golden Hour Honey, Clean & Bright
3. **Professional Studios** — Workflow Toolkit (foundation, exposure, WB, sharpening, noise)
4. **Film Photography Enthusiasts** — Color and B&W film simulations
